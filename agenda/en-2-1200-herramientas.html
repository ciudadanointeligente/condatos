Opening Data as citizens


Speakers

Sergio Araiza | SocialTIC | MX
Hugo Osorio | Gobierno Fácil | MX
Juan Pablo Escobar | Codeando | MX
Mor Rubinstein | Open Knowledge Foundation | UK
Francisco Mekler | IMCO | MX


Moderator

Paulina Bustos


Anfitriones Co-hosts
Codeando México

The Open Data movement has important differences when it is driven by government and when it is civil society that leads the effort. This panel will discuss the best practices related to opening data from the community and for the community.
