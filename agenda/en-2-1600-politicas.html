Stage 1


Similarities and differences between Open Data and Access to Public Information


Speakers

Haydee Pérez | Fundar
Romina Colman | La Nación
Fabiano Angélico | Prefectura de la Ciudad de Sao Paulo


Moderator

Silvana Fumega


Co-host
Silvana Fumega

It is necessary to explore the convergence and divergence between actors who work in areas of Open Data and access to public information within a same government to understand areas of collaboration. This panel will promote a dialogue and exchange of perspectives and experiences between different actors and sectors to advance the Open Data agenda in the region.
