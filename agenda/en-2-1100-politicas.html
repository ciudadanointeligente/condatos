Joining Forces: Building a Government by and for the People


Speakers

Marcus Dapp | OKFN + Code for Germany | GE
Preston Rhea | Code for America | US
Código CDMX | MX


Moderator

Lynn Fine


Co-host
Code for America

Collaborations between citizens and government will be explored in this panel through Code for America’s perspective. We will highlight experiences on how to gather communities to work with government, how to engage citizens, ways to foster civic technology and to convince people about the value of Open Data.
