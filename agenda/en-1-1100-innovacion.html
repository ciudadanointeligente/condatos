11:00-12:00 AM


Productivity and Open Data


Speakers

Mat Travizano | Grandata | US
Jeff Kaplan | Socrata | US
Moís Cherem | ENOVA | MX
Alex Cantú | SkyAlert | MX


Moderator

Joel Gurin


Co-host
OD500

The objective of this panel is to discuss trends in the landscape of Open Data, the data types that have proven most useful and what new and established businesses can learn from the work so far. Based on their collective experiences, the panel will show how public data being tapped to develop new opportunities and create new economic and social value.
