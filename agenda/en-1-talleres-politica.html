Making Policy Public

Escuela de Graduados en Administración Pública y Políticas Públicas | ITESM


October 1 from 1100 - 1730


Aimed at decision-makers, public administrators and government advisors, this talk will explore the use of Open Data to improve the performance of governments and raise the quality of decision-making in public policy. It focuses on improving the professional skills of the participants in the use of Open Data for government solutions.
