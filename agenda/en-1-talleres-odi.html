Open Data 101

The Open Data Institute


October 1 from 1300 - 1800


The objective of this workshop is to learn what is Open Data and the alternatives for their publication. There will be practical exercises conducted to define what is and what is not Open Data, learn about its potential benefits, risks, and how they could impact government processes.
