Open Government Plan: a roadmap for the governments of the region

Comisión Económica para América Latina


October 1 from 1100 - 1300


Its aimed at providing concepts and practical tools to enable participants to develop and implement a strategy/Open Government plan for their institutions, with an emphasis on the field of Open Data and the reuse of public sector information. It is mainly addressed to strategists for Open Government and Open Data of all levels of Public Administration in the region (central, regional, and local), representatives of civil society and other stakeholders in the ecosystem of Open Government/Open Data.
