Open Tourism

Enrique Lancis Sanjoaquín | Sociedad Estatal para la Gestión de la Innovación y las Tecnologías Turísticas | ES

Moderator

Pedro Lichtle


Co-host
SECTUR

Tourism requires the productive use of information and applied research for the design of efficient public policies and the generation of business models that increase the attraction of tourism destinations. For this, it’s necessary to use different sources of information and generate discussions on the possibility of analyzing the behavior of tourists with data and provide knowledge based on real activity, not just surveys. This analysis requires the use of Open Data for tourism as well as strong cooperation between the public, private and social sectors.
