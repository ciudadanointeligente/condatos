18:00-19:00 PM


Technology for Social Change


Speakers

Márcio Vasconcelos | AVINA | BR
Fernando Perini | International Development Research Centre
Fabrizio Scrollini | Iniciativa Latinoamericana por los Datos Abiertos
Juan Espinoza | Hivos


Anfitrión Co-host
AVINA

This panel will open debate on strategies to generate social change through the use of Open Data and civic technology. It will be focused on the characteristics of the present ecosystem through the perspective of three initiatives that work and articulate strategies in the region.
