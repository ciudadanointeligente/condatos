13:00-14:00


Multilateral support for openness


Speakers

Amparo Ballivian | Banco Mundial
Rolf Alter | Organización para la Cooperación y el Desarrollo Económico
Steven Griner | Organización de los Estados Americanos
Nicolás Dassen | Banco Interamericano de Desarrollo
Alejandra Naser | Comisión Económica para América Latina


Moderator

Claudia Calvin


This panel will feature different multilateral organizations including IDB, World Bank, IDRC, CEPAL, OECD and OAS who direct their efforts to promote Open Data and support countries by providing aid, expertise and tools to generate Open Government data strategies.
