The Internet of Things


Speakers

Vagner Diniz | World Wide Web Consortium
André Pase | Pontifícia Universidad Católica do Rio Grande do Sul
Erik Huesca | Academia Mexicana de Informática + FUNCO


Moderator

Caroline Burle


Co-host
W3C

Currently there are more devices conected to the internet than people in the world. In 2020 there will be 50 billion connected devices. This requieres web accessible, machine readable data in order for devices to connect among each other. This panel will present a conceptual approximation to the subject, followed by practical cases of Internet of Things and the presentation of IFTTT technology which allows technology automation using web tools that interact with connected hardware.
