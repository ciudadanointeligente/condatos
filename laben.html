LabConDatos

Oct 1 - Biblioteca de México 10:00 - 19:00
Oct 2 - Centro de Cultura Digital 17:00

Within the second Open Data Regional Conference Latin America and the Caribbean, the Office of the President of the Republic, Data4 and APPlícate invite you to participate as a volunteer in LabConDatos which will be held on the Biblioteca de México and the Centro de Cultura Digital on October 1 and 2.

We are looking for seven programmers, seven designers and seven data analists, that will be working, under the leadership of a mentor, to analize and generate intearctive visualizations using one of seven database released by the mexican federal government this year at datos.gob.mx.

Prize: $ 40,000 MXN


The visualizations will be realeased at condatos.org/lab.html and at datos.gob.mx/historias and the team members of the best visualization will recieve a $40,000 mexican peso price.

If you are interested in participating, send your CV and a paragraph about why you want to participate in LabConDatos to dataton@datos.gob.mx before the 25th of September


If you are chosen to participate you will recieve a message before September 26th

Requirements:

  • Programmers: experience in HTML/CSS, JQUERY, D3, MYSQL, SQLITE or ANGULAR.
  • Designers: experience with Adobe Illustrator, Indesign, Fireworks; web development experience and skills such as HTML5, CSS or WordPress.
  • Data Analists: experience in data processing and knowledge of software like Stata, R, Excel o SPSS.

Topics and datos

Maternal Mortality - GIRE A.C. Agricultural Subsidies - FUNDAR A.C. Cruzade Against Hunger - México Evalúa FONDEN - CIDE IMSS Beneficiaries - IMCO SCT - ITDP SEDESOL - GEOSOC
